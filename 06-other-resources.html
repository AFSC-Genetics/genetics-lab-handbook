7  MML, AFSC, and NOAA Resources

7.1 Public websites

The NOAA Western Regional Center site has links to information about campus facility, security, and IT services, as well as a list of NOAA offices (e.g., Pacific Marine Evironmental Laboratory [PMEL]) present at WRC.

The Alaska Fisheries Science Center website has information about AFSC Divisions, surveys, species, etc., plus tons of links to other resources, such as AFSC Publications.

NOAA Fisheries’ Marine Mammal Laboratory site has great general information about the work that we do.

The NOAA Staff Directory is a quick way to look up someone’s email, phone number, and line office. Staff can log in to the site to also view organization, position title, and employee type.

7.2 Internal sites

The AFSC Intranet is your go-to resource for announcements and events, AFSC policies and procedures, safety, funding, etc.
