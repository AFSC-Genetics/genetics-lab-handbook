1 Welcome!

Welcome to the MML Manual. This repo is intended for current staff of the Marine Mammal Laboratory at the Alaska Fisheries Science Center.

This site serves as a central hub for information on how we do our work and our expectations for our team. It is also a space to document institutional knowledge and important information about procedures and available resources. If you have suggestions for additions or changes, please contact Nancy Friday (nancy.friday@noaa.gov) or make a pull request.

This site was based on the excellent Faylab Lab Manual and developed as part of our group’s participation in the Openscapes Champions program.
